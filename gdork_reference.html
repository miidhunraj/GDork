Google Dork Operators
site:
Restrict results to a domain
site:example.com
inurl:
URL must contain the term
inurl:admin
intitle:
Page title contains term
intitle:"index of"
filetype:
Filter by file extension
filetype:sql
intext:
Body text contains term
intext:"DB_PASSWORD"
cache:
Google's cached version
cache:example.com
"phrase"
Exact phrase match
"index of /backup"
-term
Exclude a term
inurl:admin -inurl:https
OR / |
Match either term
inurl:admin OR inurl:login
*
Wildcard any word
"password * admin"
inanchor:
Link anchor text
inanchor:"login here"
related:
Similar sites
related:github.com

Pro Tips

▶Combine operators for precision: site:target.com filetype:env "DB_PASSWORD"
▶Use delay ≥ 3s to avoid Google rate-limiting (HTTP 429). Use a VPN to rotate IPs.
▶Always get written permission before dorking a target organisation's domain.
▶Combine with Shodan: dork Google for URLs, then use Shodan to probe services on those IPs.
▶Results are indexed snapshots — always verify live before reporting as a vulnerability.
▶The Exploit-DB Google Hacking Database (GHDB) has 7000+ community dorks to explore.
